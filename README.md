## Introduction

This page is all about the art of Ballet. As a classically-trained ballet dancer, I wanted to share my love of the art form by creating a Tribute Page during my Persevere Code Camp training. You can view photos of ballet dancers, listen to classicl music while you browse, learn about ballet, and link to websites of classical ballet companies. Learn about some of the greatest ballerinas ever and visit sites of my favorite dance companies.

## Usage
Scroll to view photos; and click to view videos and listen to music.
